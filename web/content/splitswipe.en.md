# Lightweight Window Management for macOS

SplitSwipe is an ultra-lightweight window management tool for macOS. No complex shortcuts or dragging required. Instantly align and cycle windows into designated zones with a single keyboard shortcut.

Designed to freely switch between 2-Way and 3-Way split modes to suit your workflow.
